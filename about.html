Who we are

Renting a home should not break the bank. We are the first to offer cheaper living in New York City. While keeping cost at a minimum we continue to provide the essentials for living. We have designed these capsule homes to become your perfect home. We have also partnered with many work forces to provide housing benefits. To find more information, please explore our homes.

Our Mission

Our goal is to help create affordable housing for those who live in the pricey city. The experience in New York City is made not only in the home but also out of the bed. By providing inexpensive housing, our mission is to encourage occupants whether they are natives or are new to the city to go out and use their expendable funds to explore their city.
